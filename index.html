Hi! I am Nilakshi!
A developer by profession and a designer by passion.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between code and design. Making applications that are intuitive for the users and finding more ways to create a natural interaction between humans and machines is what drives me.

My Work

My work has primarily been in the area of coding; websites, mobile applications, IoT systems etc. due to the nature of my degree and my profession. I have worked extensively in developing web and mobile applications keeping the needs of my client and the user in mind.

Being a full stack developer with an interest in how my apps can benefit the users, I have been involved in almost every part of the process: research, ideation,coding, testing, deployment etc. Using the principles of UX and design combined with the latest technology I strive to deliver the best.

Technology is ever-evolving, where change is the only constant, however I'm not afraid of change, there's something new to learn always and I'm open to new experiences.
